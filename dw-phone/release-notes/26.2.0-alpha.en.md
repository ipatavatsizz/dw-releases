# DW Phone — 26.2.0-alpha Release Notes

**Date**: March 1, 2026 | 🇹🇷 [Türkçe](./26.2.0-alpha.tr.md)

---

> This is the first public alpha of DW Phone 26.2. A lot has changed under the hood and on the surface — here's everything that's new, explained as if you're seeing it for the first time.

---

## 🌐 The Phone Now Speaks Your Language

Gone are the days of a single-language phone. DW Phone now ships with **full English and Turkish support** out of the box — and the architecture is ready for more languages to be added easily.

Switching languages isn't just a setting flip either. When you change your language, you'll see a brief loading screen — just like switching system language on a real device. It feels right.

> The Region setting has been removed to keep things clean. Language is what matters.

---

## 📱 Contacts & Messages — Rebuilt from Scratch

The old Contacts and Messages apps were functional, but barely. They've been completely redesigned.

**Contacts** now has:

- A dedicated **Recent Calls** tab to see who called and when
- A built-in **Keypad** for dialing numbers directly
- Full **Contact Detail** pages with call and message shortcuts

**Messages** now has:

- A modern, clean **Inbox** that actually looks like a messaging app
- A proper **Compose** screen for starting new conversations
- Updated **Group Info** pages so you always know who's in a chat

Under the hood, both apps now use a much faster **RPC architecture**. No more funny data sync issues when you have a spike in ping.

---

## 🛒 App Store — Install What You Actually Need

Apps are no longer baked into every player's phone from the start. We've added a proper **App Store**.

Install the apps you want. Uninstall the ones you don't. Your phone, your setup.

---

## 🔊 Volume HUD — Now It Feels Like a Real Phone

Press the volume buttons on the phone frame (or use your keyboard shortcuts) while on the home screen, and you'll see an **iOS-style volume bar** slide in — clean, quick, and out of your way.

Hold the button down and the volume adjusts smoothly as long as you hold it. Tap to nudge. Hold to sweep. Just like the real thing.

---

## 🎨 Themes That Actually React to the World

The phone's **Auto Theme** no longer follows your PC's light/dark setting. Instead, it follows the in-game time of day:

- **07:00** → switches to Light mode
- **19:00** → switches to Dark mode

If you're playing at night in-game, the phone looks like it's night. Immersion matters.

---

## 🎛 Control Center — Now Actually Useful

The Control Center has been overhauled with several quality-of-life improvements:

**Long-press any toggle to jump directly to its Settings page.** Wi-Fi? Long-press → straight to Wi-Fi settings. Bluetooth? Same. Every toggle now doubles as a shortcut.

**The background is now properly blurred.** It looks like glass over your world, not a dark rectangle.

**"Hotspot" has been renamed to "Nearby Share"** and now has its own dedicated Settings page.

**Do Not Disturb** has been added as a real, persistent toggle — not a fake button. Turn it on and notifications are silenced until you turn it off. It saves between sessions. Long-press to jump straight to the DND settings page.

We cleaned up the header too — the three icon buttons (Power, Edit, Settings) are gone. The Control Center does one job now: quick controls.

---

## 🔕 Do Not Disturb — Finally Persistent

This one deserves its own callout. DND isn't just a UI toggle anymore.

When you enable **Do Not Disturb**, incoming notifications are **actually suppressed** — they won't pile up in your notification panel while DND is on. Turn it off, and notifications resume as normal.

The setting is saved to your profile and restored when you log back in.

Find it in: **Settings → Do Not Disturb**

---

## 🔔 Notifications — Cleaner, Smoother

The notification panel got some polish:

When you tap **Clear** with notifications in your panel, they don't just vanish. Each notification **swipes out one by one** in a cascading animation before disappearing — satisfying and clear.

The "No notifications" empty state no longer has a clunky card background. It just sits cleanly on the blurred panel. Less visual noise.

---

## 🗂 Recent Apps — Close All, But Make It Feel Good

The "Close All" button in the Recent Apps screen used to instantly wipe everything. Now, when you tap it, each app card **flies off the screen one by one** in a staggered animation before the overlay closes. Small detail, big feel difference.

---

## 🛠 Technical Improvements

- **RPC Migration**: Net Events and NUI Callbacks across Contacts and Messages have been replaced with our custom RPC system. Better performance, fewer edge cases.
- **Database Migrations**: Schema updates now run automatically via `dw_phone_schema_migrations`. No manual DB changes needed on update.
- **Do Not Disturb Persistence**: `doNotDisturb` is now a proper column in the sessions table, saved and restored per player.
- **Gallery Infrastructure**: Backend types and database structure for the upcoming Gallery app have been added.
- **Bug Fix**: MySQL column alias quoting errors in gallery queries (`getGalleryMedia`, `toggleGalleryFavorite`, `getContacts`) have been corrected. Favorites now save and load correctly.
- **Ghost Render Fix**: Phantom UI renders during phone boot have been fully resolved.
